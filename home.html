What is DigiGigirlz ?

DigiGirlz High Tech Camp is a multiday experience that gives high school girls an in-depth look at Microsoft and careers in technology. The DigiGirlz High Tech Camp for girls works to dispel stereotypes of the high-tech industry. We continue to look for opportunities to give young people a chance to experience, firsthand, what it is like to develop cutting-edge technology. During the camp session, the girls listen to executive speakers, participate in technology tours and demonstrations, network, and learn through hands-on experience in workshops. Established in 2000, the camp program continues to grow and evolve.
